---

When [BunnyDS](/user/bunnyds/overview) is on, BunnyOS provides your market data and AI. The **spending limit** card on the *bunny data services* page is where that usage will eventually be billed and capped.

## The spending limit card

Below the on/off status card you'll find a **spending limit** card with a **monthly limit** field and an **approve spending limit** button. This is where you'll set a monthly cap once paid usage begins, so the agent is never billed more than you've approved.

BunnyDS is free for a limited time, so there's nothing to bill yet. The card is greyed out and both controls are turned off: you can turn BunnyDS on and use data and AI at $0 with no approval step.

<Note>
The turned-off spending controls only relate to paying for data and AI, not to your trading. You still need a wallet connected to trade with the agent. See [Connect your wallet](/user/getting-started/wallet-connection).
</Note>

When paid tiers arrive, the **monthly limit** you approve becomes the ceiling: usage is billed against it from your wallet, up to the cap you set.

<Warning>
Prices and tiers aren't finalized while BunnyDS is free. Treat the spending controls as a preview of what's coming, not a live billing screen.
</Warning>

If BunnyDS isn't available on your deployment, bring your own provider keys instead. See [Getting data: managed vs your own keys](/user/getting-started/data-and-keys).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Will I be charged while BunnyDS is free?">
    No. The spending controls are turned off and nothing is billed from your wallet for BunnyDS usage. Your normal trading still uses your own funds as usual.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I type a monthly limit?">
    The field is intentionally turned off while it's free. There's nothing to cap yet, so the input and the approve button stay off until paid tiers go live.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I avoid BunnyDS billing entirely?">
    Yes. See [Managed vs your own keys](/user/getting-started/data-and-keys) for running data and AI on your own provider keys.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
