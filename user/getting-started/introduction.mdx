bunnyOS is the **action social network** on [Base](https://base.org). Instead of trading on chat and rumors, you follow **bunnies**: tradable sources of on-chain alpha. You buy a bunny's keys to unlock its recommendations, and the agent turns each call into a transaction you approve. Nothing moves without your signature.

## How bunnyOS works

The whole product is one loop, from a tradable signal to an on-chain action:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Trade bunnies on bunnyDEX">
    A **bunny** is a source of action recommendations, run by a person, a team, an algorithm, or an AI. Each bunny has its own **keys** that you buy and sell on a bonding curve, priced in `$OS`. See [Buy & sell bunny keys](/user/bunnyex/trading).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock its recommendations">
    Holding a bunny's keys unlocks its gated [**recommendations**](/user/bunnyex/recommendations) and community [**threads**](/user/bunnyex/threads). The stronger a bunny's track record, the more its keys tend to be worth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Act from your inbox">
    Every recommendation from a bunny you hold gathers in your [Actions inbox](/user/actions/inbox), next to alerts and one-click moves from your own 24/7 [actions](/user/actions/builder). One click hands a call to the agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve in your wallet">
    The agent reads live data, prepares the transaction, and sends it to your Base wallet to sign. You are the final authority on every on-chain action.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Non-custodial by design

<Steps>
  <Step title="A call comes in">
    A recommendation lands in your inbox, or you ask the agent directly for a swap, transfer, or research request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The agent prepares">
    bunnyOS reads live data and assembles the transaction, then shows you exactly what it intends to do.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You approve in your own wallet">
    The transaction is sent to your connected Base wallet, where you sign it yourself or reject it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  bunnyOS never holds your funds and nothing moves without your signature. Always review a transaction in your wallet before signing: you are the final authority on every on-chain action.
</Warning>

## How to get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to Base">
    From the landing page, choose **connect to base** to sign in with your Base wallet. See [Sign in with your Base wallet](/user/getting-started/wallet-connection) for the full walkthrough.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the terminal">
    Once connected, select **open terminal** to enter bunnyOS and its tabbed surfaces.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse bunnies and buy a key">
    Open [bunnyDEX](/user/bunnyex/trading), find a bunny whose calls you want to follow, and buy a key. Its recommendations then start flowing into your [Actions inbox](/user/actions/inbox).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Accordion title="Do I need any keys to start?">
  No. bunnyOS works out of the box with the managed **bunnyCompute** option. See [Getting data: managed vs your own keys](/user/getting-started/data-and-keys).
</Accordion>
