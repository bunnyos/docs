Chat is how every call reaches the agent. Most of the time a recommendation from a [bunny you hold](/user/bunnyex/recommendations) or one of your [actions](/user/actions/inbox) pre-fills it for you, but you can open it any time to ask in plain language. The agent proposes; you stay the final authority on anything that touches your funds.

Make sure you're signed in first, see [Sign in with your Base wallet](/user/getting-started/wallet-connection) if you haven't connected yet.

## Open the floating chat

On every terminal page a launcher sits in the **bottom-right corner**: a rounded button with the Bunny icon and an **`open agent`** label. Select it and a chat panel slides up over the page; the button then reads **`close`**. There's one shared conversation across the whole terminal, so opening chat on any tab resumes where you left off, and closing the panel does not stop a request that is already running.

<Tip>
  When the panel is closed and the agent is still working, a small pulsing green dot appears on the launcher. Reopen it any time to watch the rest stream in.
</Tip>

## What you can ask

Type in the input box (placeholder: **`type your instructions here`**) and press **Enter** to send, or **Shift + Enter** for a new line. Bunny interprets what you want, pulls whatever data it needs, and either answers directly or proposes a transaction.

| You can ask | What Bunny does |
| --- | --- |
| `show my portfolio` | Reads your connected wallet and lists your live holdings |
| `research a token` | Looks up live market data and security findings for a token |
| `buy 10 $OS` | Prepares the swap and hands it to your wallet to sign |
| `set a price alert` | Sets up an automated action that watches for your condition |

<Note>
  Bunny can only interface with the protocols and services you've enabled. An empty conversation shows the reminder: *"bunnyOS can interface directly with protocols and services you enable."* See [Getting data: managed vs your own keys](/user/getting-started/data-and-keys) for how to power the agent.
</Note>

## Watch live progress

As Bunny works, a status line under its message shows what it is doing right now: **`thinking…`**, **`running {tool}…`** (the active tool name fills in, for example `running get_portfolio…`), or **`responding…`**. When the turn ends it settles on **`done`** (green), or **`stopped`** if the response was interrupted, so you are never left guessing.

## Approve on-chain actions

When a request needs a signature, the agent builds the transaction and surfaces an approval button right in the chat, labeled **`approve transaction in base app →`** (or **`open base app to approve →`**).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review what Bunny proposes">
    Read the agent's message so you know exactly what is being signed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve in your Base app">
    Select the button to open your Base app's approval screen, then approve or reject, exactly as you would for any wallet request. If your browser blocks the popup, it falls back to a new tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track confirmation">
    Back in chat, click **`click here once done →`** and Bunny checks the status: **`congrats — transaction confirmed`** (with a **`view on basescan ↗`** link when available), **`still pending — check your wallet`**, or **`transaction failed`**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Nothing moves without your signature. See [What is bunnyOS?](/user/getting-started/introduction) for the non-custodial model.
</Warning>

## Saved history

Your conversations are saved automatically. Use **`new chat`** in the panel header to start fresh, or **`history`** to reopen a past conversation (the count of **`saved chats`** shows next to it). Each saved chat lists its title and last-updated time; hover one to delete it.
