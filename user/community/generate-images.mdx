---
title: Generate bunny images
description: Turn a text prompt into an on-brand bunny mascot image and share it on the public bunny wall.
---

Describe a bunny, and bunnyOS creates an on-brand mascot image from your words. Every image you make is posted to a shared public gallery called the **bunny wall**, so you can browse what everyone else has dreamed up too.

<Frame caption="The bunny wall, where new creations appear newest first.">
  <img src="/images/app/bunny-wall.png" alt="The bunny wall showing generated bunny images with their prompts" />
</Frame>

## Make a bunny image

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the generator">
    Go to **Settings** › **Community** and choose **Generate**. You can also press **Cmd/Ctrl+I** from anywhere in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your bunny">
    Type a short description of the bunny you want, such as an astronaut bunny or a bunny sipping coffee.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create it">
    Press **Generate**, or **Cmd/Ctrl+Enter**, and wait a moment for your image to appear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>You need to be signed in to generate images. There is also a daily limit on how many you can make, so it resets each day.</Note>

## Save your creations

You can download any image you make and keep it on your device. Use it as a profile picture, a sticker, or wherever you like.

## The bunny wall

Every image you generate is added to the public bunny wall automatically. It shows everyone's creations with the newest first, and each one is tagged with the creator's shortened wallet address and the prompt that made it. Scroll through for inspiration before you write your own prompt.

<Warning>The bunny wall is public. Anything you generate is visible to everyone, so keep your prompts friendly.</Warning>
