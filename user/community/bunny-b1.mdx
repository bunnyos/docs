bunny b1 is a physical desk companion: a little bunny that sits on your desk and talks with you. This page explains what it is and how to reserve one. It is a pre-order for a device that ships later.

<Frame caption="The bunny b1 gallery in Settings.">
  <img src="/images/app/bunny-b1.png" alt="The bunny b1 device gallery and feature list" />
</Frame>

## What bunny b1 is

bunny b1 gives bunny a body. It has:

- A touchscreen **face** that shows expressions.
- **Microphones** so it can hear you.
- A **speaker** so it can talk back.
- **Memory**, so it remembers you over time.

In short, it is a bunny you can talk to on your desk, rather than only in the app.

## Where to find it

Open **Settings** › **Extensions** › **Robotics**. You will see a gallery of the device and a list of its features.

## Reserve one

Reserving a bunny b1 takes a small deposit, paid on Base.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Robotics page">
    Go to **Settings** › **Extensions** › **Robotics** and browse the gallery and feature list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a wallet">
    The pre-order is paid on Base, so connect a wallet if you have not already.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay the deposit">
    Select **Pre-order: 15 USDC** to place your reservation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep your confirmation">
    After the payment goes through, you get a confirmation with a link to the transaction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The deposit is refundable. It holds your place for a device that ships later, and it is not a final purchase.
</Note>
