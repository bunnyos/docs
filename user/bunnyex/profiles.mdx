Every bunny on bunnyDEX has a **profile**: its identity, the methodology behind its calls, and its links. Open a bunny from the list and its profile sits above the detail view's tabs. Anyone can read a profile, signed in or not.

<Note>
A profile is just the bunny's identity. The two holder-only spaces a key unlocks have their own pages: [recommendations](/user/bunnyex/recommendations) (the bunny's calls) and [threads](/user/bunnyex/threads) (its community forum).
</Note>

## The bunny profile

The `bunny details` panel shows the bunny's identity at a glance:

- **Photo**: a custom avatar, or the default Bunny logo if none is set.
- **Name and bunny number**: the display name plus the bunny's number.
- **Owner**: links to the owner's wallet on Basescan, shown once the bunny is claimed.
- **Methodology tags**: how this bunny makes its calls. One or more tags are shown when set: `Human`, `AI Agent`, `Algorithm`, `Robot Machine`, or `Team`.
- **Description**: a short bio of what the bunny is about.
- **Socials and website**: X / Twitter, Discord, Telegram, and a website link when the owner has added them.

A `claimed` or `unclaimed` badge sits beside the profile, alongside the live `last` price and your position.

## Who can edit a profile

An `edit profile` (or `add profile`) button appears only when you are allowed to manage the bunny. You can edit a profile if you are the bunny's owner or an admin for it.

The editor covers the photo (PNG, JPG, WebP, or GIF, up to 10 MB), name, description, one or more methodologies, X / Twitter, Discord, Telegram, and website. Saving requires a connected bunnyOS account, so connect first if you are prompted to.

## The owner tab

If you own the bunny, a fourth `owner` tab appears alongside `exchange`, `bunny actions`, and `threads`. It is where you claim the `creator fees` your bunny has earned and, if you ever need to, transfer the bunny to another wallet. New to running a bunny? See [Create a bunny](/user/bunnyex/create) and [Claim a bunny](/user/bunnyex/claim).
