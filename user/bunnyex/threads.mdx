title: "Bunny threads"
description: "Join a bunny's holder-only community forum: post, reply, and vote."
---

Every bunny has **threads**: a holder-only community forum on its detail view. In the app it is the `threads` tab, alongside `exchange` and `bunny actions`.

<Note>
Threads are holders only. To post or join in, [connect your bunnyOS account](/user/configure/bunnyos-account) and hold at least one key of that bunny. Without a key the tab shows a **threads locked** notice; [buy a key](/user/bunnyex/trading) to unlock it.
</Note>

## Browse, post, and vote

<Steps>
<Step title="Browse threads">
Sort posts by `new` or `top`. Each post shows its score, comment count, author, and time.
</Step>
<Step title="Start a post">
Choose `new post`, add a title and body, then `post`.
</Step>
<Step title="Reply and vote">
Open a post to read it, reply inline (replies nest a couple of levels deep), and up- or down-vote posts and comments.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Moderation

The owner and moderators can delete any post or comment, and can open `manage` to edit the moderator and ban lists. If you have been banned from a community, you will see a notice and will not be able to post.
