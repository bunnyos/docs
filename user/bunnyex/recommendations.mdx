---
title: "Bunny recommendations"
description: "Open a bunny's holder-only recommendations feed and execute its calls with one click."
---

Every bunny has a **recommendations** feed: the calls its owner posts for the people who hold its keys. In the app it is the `bunny actions` tab on the bunny's detail view, between `exchange` and `threads`.

<Note>
The feed is holders only. To open it, [connect your bunnyOS account](/user/configure/bunnyos-account) and hold at least one key of that bunny. Without a key the tab shows a lock icon and a **recommendations locked** notice; [buy a key](/user/bunnyex/trading) to unlock it.
</Note>

## Read the feed

Filter the feed by `active`, `completed`, or `all`. Each entry is one of two kinds:

| Kind | What you can do |
| --- | --- |
| Text suggestion | **Execute** (sends the suggestion into chat so you can act on it) or **dismiss**. |
| Recorded call | Display-only. There is no execute action. |

Executing or dismissing marks the item **done** for you only and moves it to `completed`. This is just your personal view: executing hands the suggestion to chat, where the agent helps you carry it out. Nothing happens automatically, and you approve every transaction in your own Base wallet.

<Tip>
Recommendations from every bunny you hold are also gathered into your [Actions inbox](/user/actions/inbox) under **from bunnies**, so you don't have to open each bunny to catch up.
</Tip>

## For owners: posting and archiving

If you own the bunny, you post recommendations from its `bunny actions` tab, and each one carries an `archive` control (with `unarchive` to bring it back). Archiving hides a recommendation from the active feed for everyone without deleting it. The inbox also drops recommendations after a few days, so stale ones don't linger.
