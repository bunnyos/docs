---
title: "Claim a bunny"
description: "Verify your X account to take ownership of an unclaimed bunny on bunnyDEX."
---

When a bunny is [created](/user/bunnyex/create) for an X account, it starts **`unclaimed`**: its keys trade, but no one owns it yet. **Claiming** proves you control that X account and hands ownership of the bunny to your wallet. Once you own it, you can edit its profile, post recommendations, and collect its creator fees.

<Note>
You need a [connected Base account](/user/getting-started/wallet-connection). Claiming is open to anyone who controls the bunny's X account; there is no approval step.
</Note>

## Spot an unclaimed bunny

An unclaimed bunny shows an **`unclaimed`** badge. Open it, and if it is linked to an X account its detail view shows a banner: *"this bunny is linked to @handle and hasn't been claimed."* with a **`claim this bunny`** button.

## Link your X account

To claim, your wallet must be linked to the bunny's X account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the X manager">
    Select **`link x`** in the top-right of the page header. The panel reads *"sign in with x to claim a bunny."*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with X">
    Select **`sign in with x`** and authorize bunnyOS on X. You are sent to X and back. Your linked accounts then appear under **`linked x accounts`**, and you can **`unlink`** any time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You can skip ahead and press **`claim this bunny`** first. If your wallet isn't linked yet, bunnyOS prompts you (*"link the @handle x account to your wallet to claim this bunny."*), opens the X sign-in for you, and finishes the claim automatically when you return.
</Tip>

## Claim it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the bunny">
    Go to the unclaimed bunny on bunnyDEX.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim this bunny">
    Select **`claim this bunny`**. bunnyOS checks that you have the X account linked, then transfers ownership to your wallet. There is no gas to pay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're the owner">
    The badge flips to **`claimed`** and your wallet shows as the owner.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What claiming unlocks

Once you own a bunny, a fourth **`owner`** tab appears next to `exchange`, `bunny actions`, and `threads`, and you can:

- **Edit the profile** with the **`edit profile`** button. See [Bunny profiles](/user/bunnyex/profiles).
- **Post recommendations** to your key holders. See [Bunny recommendations](/user/bunnyex/recommendations).
- **Collect creator fees** your bunny has earned, and **transfer** the bunny to another wallet, from the `owner` tab.
